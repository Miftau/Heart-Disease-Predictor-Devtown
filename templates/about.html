About HeartPredict

Transforming heart health with AI-powered diagnostics

HeartPredict is an AI-powered diagnostic tool designed to help healthcare professionals and individuals assess the likelihood of heart disease. By combining clinical parameters and modern data science, it transforms traditional diagnosis into fast, data-driven insights.

Try Our Prediction

Our Story

HeartPredict was founded in 2025 by a team of cardiologists and data scientists who recognized the critical need for early detection tools in cardiovascular health. Our journey began when we noticed that many heart conditions could be prevented with early intervention, yet traditional diagnostic methods were often too late.

Combining decades of medical expertise with cutting-edge machine learning techniques, we developed a platform that makes predictive cardiology accessible to both healthcare providers and individuals. Our mission is to reduce heart disease mortality by providing accurate, early detection tools that empower preventive care.

Today, HeartPredict serves thousands of users globally, helping them take proactive steps toward better heart health. Our commitment remains unchanged: to leverage technology for the betterment of human health.

Our Technology

Built using Flask, Scikit-learn, and Plotly, HeartPredict leverages machine learning models trained on cardiology datasets to deliver real-time predictions and interactive visualizations.

  • Advanced ML algorithms trained on 10,000+ patient records
  • Real-time risk assessment with 95% accuracy
  • Secure, HIPAA-compliant data handling
  • Interactive visualizations for easy interpretation
